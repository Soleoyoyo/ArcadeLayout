Help & Tips

Welcome to the Arcade Layout Planner!

  • 🕹️ Add Cabinets using the cabinet creator on the left - Input the name, dimensions and chosen colour of your cabinet.
  • 🎨 Edit Cabinet Details using the bottom panel — Adjust the name, dimensions, rotation and colour.
  • 📏 Snap to Grid or align cabinets edge-to-edge — You can toggle snapping anytime.
  • 🔒 Lock cabinets in place so you can't accidentally move them - Toggle locking on any cabinet of your choosing.
  • ➕ Duplicate any cabinet by clicking the duplicate button.
  • ❌ Delete unwanted cabinets by either pressing the delete button or your delete key.
  • 💾 Save Cabinets locally to your computer by using the Save Current Cabinet button on the left on any selected cabinet - These will stay saved even if you reset your room.
  • 📤 Export and Import Cabinets of your choice by using the menu at the bottom left - You can even share these between others by sending and receiving cabinet files.
  • 🏁 Change you Room dimensions by using the top menu on the right - You can alter the width and length of your room as well as the size of the grid. Click update to apply these new measurements to your room.
  • 💾 Save Rooms you are working on so you can come back to them later - You can also save multiple rooms and swap between them by loading a room.
  • 📤 Share Rooms by exporting and importing rooms - All rooms will be downloaded to your computer. You can also download your room as a png.
  • 🔍 Zoom In and Out on your room by using the buttons to the top right of the room view - You can also use the arrow keys to move around your room when zoomed in.
  • 👾 Arcade Mode livens up your work space with neon colours and flashing lights - just press the Arcade Mode button up the top right. You can always come back to the simpler look.
  • 💡 Light Mode can be swapped to at any time - You can always come back to the dark side if the bright lights hurt your eyes.
